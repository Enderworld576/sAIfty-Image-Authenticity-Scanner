    AI Inbreeding Prevention

    Dataset Protection Workflow

    01

    Quarantine high risk

    Images marked high risk should be removed from training datasets until a human reviewer approves them.

    02

    Review uncertainty

    Uncertain images should be manually reviewed because mixed signals can happen in real photos and edited media.

    03

    Preserve clean data

    Clean data improves model reliability, reduces retraining costs, and lowers the risk of AI inbreeding loops.

    Process

    How It Works

    • The image is checked for camera metadata and known generator fingerprints.
    • Pixels are analyzed for texture smoothness, frequency patterns, repeated patches, edge behavior, compression noise, and color balance.
    • Scores are combined into an AI probability using weighted signal fusion.
    • The result helps protect AI training data from AI inbreeding.

    Boundaries

    Limitations

    • This is an educational prototype and does not guarantee perfect detection.
    • Some real photos may lack metadata, especially after social media sharing.
    • Some AI images may include fake metadata or post-processing artifacts.
    • Best results come from combining automated scoring with human review.
