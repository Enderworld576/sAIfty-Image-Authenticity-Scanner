    Process

    How It Works

    • The browser reads the image pixels with an HTML canvas.
    • The scanner estimates metadata, texture, edge, compression, color, and pattern-risk signals.
    • High scores mean higher AI-generation risk for that signal.
    • The goal is training data safety: high-risk images should be reviewed before entering a dataset.
    !

    Limitations

    Standalone Prototype

    • This HTML copy uses a trained detector plus local explanation signals.
    • It cannot reliably read full camera EXIF metadata in every browser.
    • Real photos can be flagged and AI images can pass, so human review still matters.
    • Best results come from combining automated scoring with human review.
